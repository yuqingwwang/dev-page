About Me

Hey there, I'm Yuqing, a Software Developer with a background in Data Science. I code in Python, JavaScript and Go.

Following the completion of my Master's degree in Urban Development Planning at UCL, I started my journey of Web Development in 2022.

I've enjoyed sharing knowledge at Le Wagon's bootcamps and forming meaningful connections with diverse individuals. I take pride in listening to their stories and blending this insight with my technical skills.

As a natural team player and quick learner, I am actively searching for a Software Developer role where I can bring value and innovation to a dynamic company.

Why Web Development?

My tech journey began with learning Data Science, driven by my love for Python. However, I quickly realized that I wanted to work on something more practical and meaningful. I wanted to build something that people can interact with and enjoy.

As a result, I delved into responsive web design through freeCodeCamp and completed The Odin Project's introduction track. I have also recently finished the University of Helsinki's Full Stack Open course. Currently, I am training with Founders and Coders to become a full-stack developer.
