About Me

Hey there, I'm Yuqing, a software developer with a background in Ed Tech. I code in Python, JavaScript and Go.

Following the completion of my Master's degree in Urban Development Planning at UCL, I started my journey of web development in 2022.

With a deep passion for learning, I aspire to utilize my technical abilities to craft visually appealing and highly functional web applications.

Why Web Development?

My tech journey began with learning Data Science, driven by my love for Python. However, I quickly realised that I wanted to work on something more practical and enjoyable, beyond cleaning data and training models.

As a result, I delved into responsive web design through freeCodeCamp and completed The Odin Project's introduction track. I have also recently finished the University of Helsinki's Full Stack Open course.

But the journey into Web Development is not just about acquiring new skills. I'm also eager to connect with like-minded individuals and collaborate with them on projects. As a member of Founders and Coders' cohort 28, I'm looking forward to continuing to learn and grow with the community.
